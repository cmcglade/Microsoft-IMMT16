Personal Project: Vem Canadá

When moving to Canada, I decided to make this experience public and leverage it as a digital marketing learning tool. In October, 2015, Vem Canadá was born as a joint project with my husband to tell other Brazilians about our adventure. It started as a blog and YouTube channel and its presence in social media has been increasing ever since. Although it is a joint project, I am responsible for strategy development, community management, content creation and design.

The blog intercalates blog posts and videos every week. Since the engagement and type of content is different depending on the media, I chose to focus the most detailed content in the textual format, especially to leverage it as an opportunity for SEO.

However, even video contents are displayed on the blog along with a long description to give the opportunity to those who choose not to watch videos to enjoy the content and not miss any information.
